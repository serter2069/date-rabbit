# Structured Summary: DateRabbit

**PROJECT_NAME:** DateRabbit

**PROJECT_TYPE:** Mobile app (Marketplace/Platform)

**PROBLEM:** Modern dating apps waste users' time without compensation and lack barriers for serious intentions. Users spend effort on dates with no guarantee of commitment or seriousness from the other party.

**TARGET_AUDIENCE:** Primary - Women 25-40 years old (pragmatic daters, professionals); Secondary - Men 30-50 years old (serious seekers, efficiency-focused)

**KNOWN_COMPETITORS:** TaskRabbit (business model reference for paid services marketplace)

**MONETIZATION:** 
- 20% commission on all transactions
- Subscription plans for men
- Premium features
- Target ARR: $1.08M after 12 months
- Unit economics: $18.94 net revenue per date

**KEY_FEATURES:**
- Phase 1 (Core Marketplace): Profile creation, paid date listings, booking system, payment processing, basic chat
- Phase 2 (Trust & Growth): Reviews/ratings, verification system, calendar integration, advanced search/filters
- Phase 3 (Retention): Subscription tiers, repeat date discounts, featured profiles, AI matching suggestions

**TECH_STACK:**
- Frontend: React Native + Expo
- Backend: NestJS + PostgreSQL
- Payments: Stripe Connect

**USP:** TaskRabbit-style model applied to dating - women get compensated for their time, men pay for guaranteed serious connections. Creates economic barrier ensuring serious intentions while empowering women through monetization of dating effort.
