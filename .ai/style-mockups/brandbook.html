Guidelines

Design principles and best practices.

Design Principles

✓ Do
Use soft gradients sparingly for accents
Keep plenty of white space
Use Fraunces for headlines, DM Sans for body
Maintain consistent border-radius (12-20px)
Use subtle shadows for elevation
Keep UI elements clean and minimal
✗ Don't
Overuse pink gradients everywhere
Use harsh borders or shadows
Mix too many font weights
Crowd content with tight spacing
Use pure black (#000000)
Add unnecessary decorative elements

Brand Voice

Warm & Welcoming

Friendly but professional. We're here to help you find meaningful connections.

Clear & Direct

Simple language, no jargon. Users should always know what to expect.

Safe & Trustworthy

Security and privacy are priorities. We communicate this through every interaction.
